Privacy Policy for Tap Rush

Last updated: 6 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Tap Rush ("we", "us", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using Tap Rush you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: when you create an account, we collect your email address and a username, through Google Firebase Authentication.
  • Gameplay data: scores, levels, in-game coins, cosmetics, and progress, stored via Google Cloud Firestore so your profile and leaderboard standing are saved and synced across devices.
  • Advertising identifiers: when ads are shown, our ad provider (Google AdMob) may access your device's advertising ID and related data to deliver and measure ads.
  • Purchase data: if you buy in-app items, the purchase is processed by Google Play Billing. We store a receipt record (product purchased, date) for support and fraud-prevention purposes. We do not collect or store your payment card details.
  • Diagnostic data: crash reports and basic device information collected via Google Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and secure your account and verify your email.
  • To save your progress and display leaderboards.
  • To show and measure advertisements.
  • To process in-app purchases.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve the game.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Crashlytics, App Check) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob (advertising) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing (purchases) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Resend (transactional sign-up emails) — resend.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. Advertising and Consent

We show rewarded advertisements that you choose to watch in exchange for in-game rewards. For users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we present a consent request (using Google's User Messaging Platform) before personalized ads are shown, in line with the GDPR. You may choose non-personalized ads, and you can change your choice at any time from the privacy settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Tap Rush is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your account and gameplay data for as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below; we will delete it within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including authenticated access and server-side security rules, to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to safeguard your information.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
